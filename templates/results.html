CO2 pipeline

With the CO2 Next project, the goal is to build terminals in the Port of Rotterdam where CO2 is converted from a gas to a liquid. This liquid CO2 will than be transported by land or waterway to consumers who are not connected to CO2 pipelines. CO2 Next will be connected with projects like Aramis to get their CO2. In the initial phase of the project it is expected that the terminal capacity is approximately 3.8 Mt per year (CO2 Next, 2022).
